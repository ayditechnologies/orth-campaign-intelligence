Campaign
Intelligence

Agronomic decision windows mapped across 10 markets. Reach growers when the season is already asking them to act.

Urgency already
exists in the field.

Growers are not equally reachable all year. Decision density concentrates into short, high-pressure windows — disease onset, harvest timing, planting decisions. Outside those windows, the product's value is real but abstract. Inside them, it is immediately necessary.

Campaign Intelligence maps those windows across 100 crops in 10 markets, identifies the campaign sweet spot before each one opens, and surfaces exceptional incoming weather that changes the timing of decisions. It is built for the people allocating spend, not for the people in the field.

The hypothesis: spend aligned to decision pressure converts differently than off-season content. The tool exists to test it.
